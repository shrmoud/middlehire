The Founder

She realized the dream to make this world a better place!

Kay Garland

Founder, UrbanConnected

I was born and raised in Flint, MI. Growing up in a household with one parent all of my life felt different. It was also one of the most economically depressed times in the country. I learned to survive in a rough neighborhood. Gun shots lit up the city like fireworks. I grew up around crack houses, gangs, and abandoned houses. Getting chased by pit bulls and hearing police sirens while doing homework was a regular. I knew that if I believe and trust in God, he would not only help me but also my city.

Every day, we would play double dutch or curb ball. We met up early morning to ride our bikes, and only went back inside for lunch and dinner. The neighborhoods were filled with love, in the midst of poverty. There was still a sense of community the time. Over time, I witnessed Flint drastically shift from a thriving economy with the auto industry to a deteriorating city. I graduated college with a degree in International Business, and a minor Spanish. I’ve been privileged to volunteer by teaching children in urban communities, working with Detroit public schools to create programs for underprivileged children. I have taught abroad in Japan, Germany, and the Florida Keys with Camp Adventure. I support and partner with many agencies aimed toward empowering the children of Flint.

I desire to use my experience and education toward rebuilding communities in Flint. My passion is to make Flint a great place to raise a family and help revitalize the culture. I want to open a youth center for children. It would offer quality educational programs, foreign language classes, practical life skills training, and workshops to assist with college enrollment. Above all, I want to help children walk their purpose and realize their highest potential.

My goal is to launch a new platform - A digital hub of information dedicated to urban communities. This platform will make it easier to find and partner with other like-minded people. A portion of all proceeds will go directly to support urban communities. I want to work with the organization to not only help Flint, MI, but also to help empower other urban communities, like mine, partnering with other like-minded people whose passion is to connect and serve the greatest needs. I am ready to embark upon a rewarding journey that will promote lasting change for our future. I want to intertwine local and global organizations. In today's works, globalization is on the rise and this platform wants to explore that opportunity.

Today, as a 36-year-old woman, I have discovered what it is that God has called me to be, and that is to help our generation. I now understand that the change I prayed for is within myself. I believe that I can bless someone by sharing my story and help overcome any obstacles that someone is facing today.

Will you consider partnering with us to help us take this to the next level?
